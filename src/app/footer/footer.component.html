BLOOD DONOR MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

An Efficient System for managing blood bank and donors. It provides features such as Request for Blood, Donor Registration, Blood Bank Locator. Real time statistics and a rich dashboard. we help people around Kerala who are in need of blood donors and platelet donors. Also we help blood banks and hospitals in Kerala for meeting their blood, platelet and plasma requirements.
